No Escaping The Horde


Link: Itch.io
Year: 2024
Tools: Unity, VSCode, C#, Aseprite
Team Size: Solo
Timeframe: 3 Months
Contributions: All except sound, some art built off assets
No Escaping The Horde is a survivorslike/bullet heaven game I made for a game mechanics course in 2024. It is built around a unique health mechanic where your health is always being drained and getting damaged makes it drain faster. After completing the project, it was accepted as the only solo-dev game into 2025's NextGen Showcase; a curated exhibit of 10 student-made games.
