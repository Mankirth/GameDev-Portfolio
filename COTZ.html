Champ of the Zone


Tools: Unity (w/NGO & Relay), VSCode, C#, Aseprite
Team Size: Solo
Timeframe: 8 Months (in progress)
Contributions: All except sound
Champ of the Zone is a 2D fighting game meant to give beginners in the genre access to higher level mindgames without needing to build executional skill. It is my first online game and is made unique with its approach to characters and combat. Players create their chracter builds by assigning attacks to their binds, letting them find synergies and stacking benefits between attacks. Combat is closer to fencing where rounds are decided in one hit to emphasize the importance of reading your opponent and setting up plays.
